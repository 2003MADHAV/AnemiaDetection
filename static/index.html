Anemia Detection

Anemia is a medical condition characterized by a deficiency of healthy red blood cells or a reduction in the amount of hemoglobin in the blood. Symptoms may include fatigue, weakness, and more.

Average Range of Hemoglobin and MCV (in units):

Gender Hemoglobin MCV
Male 12.0 - 15.0 g/dL 80.0 - 95.0 fL
Female 11.5 - 14.5 g/dL 82.0 - 98.0 fL
